Making these delicious brownines will be loads of fun. First you will want to gather all the ingreedients and supplies that will be used.

  • Brownie Mix
  • Vegitable Oil 1/3 cup
  • Water 3 table spoons
  • Eggs 2
  • Pam Spray
  • Baking Pan
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Spaula for Baking
